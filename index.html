Case study · F&B · Singapore

Two workflows. Both fixed.

Reservations were managed through a WhatsApp group — staff had to manually field every booking, confirm by message, and update a shared note nobody trusted. Inventory was a daily Excel ritual that took the ops manager an hour each morning and was always one missed entry away from a stockout.

We replaced both. Reservations now run on a self-booking web app — customers pick their slot, get an automatic confirmation, and staff see everything on a live dashboard. Inventory moved from daily manual counts to real-time tracking that updates as stock moves.
